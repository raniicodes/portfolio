A little about me

I'm Rania Bajwa, a Computer Science student, self taught designer, and builder from Lahore, Pakistan. Currently studying at Goldsmiths, University of London; BSc Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning; Class of 2028.

I'm experienced in JavaScript, p5.js, HTML & CSS for building interactive interfaces, and bring a strong eye for design through Figma and the Adobe Suite — covering UI/UX, graphic design, and digital media.

When I'm not coding or designing, I'm probably exploring new coffee shops in Lahore, chasing cats, or learning about whatever's new in tech and design. I believe the best digital experiences live at the intersection of craft and curiosity.
